## Key Attributes by Category

### Switches
- Port count and speed (1G, 2.5G, 10G, 25G, 40G, 100G, 400G)
- PoE support and budget (802.3af/at/bt, up to 3600W)
- Layer 2/3 capabilities
- Stacking support
- Switching capacity (Gbps)
- Form factor (1U, 2U, modular)

### Firewalls
- Firewall throughput (Mbps/Gbps)
- IPS/NGFW/Threat protection throughput
- Concurrent sessions
- VPN tunnels
- Port configuration
- SD-WAN and ZTNA support

### Compute
- CPU sockets and max cores
- Max RAM capacity
- Drive bays and form factors
- GPU support and max count
- PCIe slots
- Management (iLO, iDRAC, CIMC)

### Wireless
- Wi-Fi generation (Wi-Fi 5/6/6E/7)
- Radio configuration (2x2, 4x4, 8x8)
- Max throughput (Mbps)
- Bands supported (2.4/5/6 GHz)
- PoE requirements
- Form factor (indoor/outdoor)

### Routers
- Throughput capacity
- WAN port configuration
- Routing protocol support
- SD-WAN capabilities
- Form factor
